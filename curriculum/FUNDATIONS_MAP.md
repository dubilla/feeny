# Fundations ↔ Feeny alignment map

The single source every Fundations-initiative slice (F1–F6) cites. Maps the
*publicly documented* Wilson Fundations® scope & sequence — at the skill-family
level, never unit-by-unit reproduction — onto Feeny's reading bands.

**IP guardrail (repeat of the backlog rule):** we align to the public scope &
sequence and to general Orton-Gillingham practice. No Wilson materials, card
art, keyword set, or trade dress; the name "Fundations" never appears in
kid-facing copy. Parent-facing copy: "follows the phonics sequence used in
most classrooms (Orton-Gillingham style)."

## The spine

Letter-keyword-sound anchors → CVC (with phoneme tapping) → digraphs &
bonus letters → glued sounds → blends → syllable types (closed → VCe →
open → r-controlled → vowel teams), with a parallel **trick words** track
of high-frequency irregulars running alongside from early Level 1.

## Decisions of record

- **Sight words = trick words (2026-07-11).** Band 4's `read-u-sight` stays
  where it is and becomes the anchor of the F4 trick-words track — Fundations
  runs trick words *parallel* to L1 phonics, so band-4 placement is
  pedagogically right and existing kid progress on the unit is preserved.
- **Glued sounds before blends.** Fundations L1 teaches digraphs → bonus
  letters + glued *all/am/an* → *ng/nk* glued families → blends; glued sounds
  are taught as whole units precisely because kids can't yet blend nasalized
  clusters sound-by-sound. Hence `read-glued` in band 4, `read-blends` in
  band 5. The *ng/nk* families sit at the band 4/5 boundary (F3).
- **Word Detective moved to band 6 (F1).** Vocabulary-in-context belongs in
  Fluency Falls; also keeps band 5 from ballooning when F6 lands.
- **Stable IDs.** Re-sequencing never changes unit/lesson/exercise/skill ids —
  kid progress is keyed by them. Skills move bands *with* their units
  (`read-digraphs`→b4, `read-sentences`→b5) so the review engine's
  band-eligibility filter matches the map.
